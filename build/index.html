Why choose us

Storeplex is a decentralised marketplace that allows anyone to buy and sell products or services for cryptocurrencies on a global scale. The marketplace will support thousands of merchants and millions of listings, with its primary focus on no fees.

Our users are not charged an insertion fee for creating listings, which gives our marketplace worldwide accessibility. Purchases made using the PLEX token benefit from no processing fees, whilst all other supported payment methods incur only a 1% processing fee.

What is Plex Token

PLEX is the marketplace’s primary currency which utilises the ERC20 Ethereum token standard. It can be used to purchase goods and services on the Storeplex platform. Every listing on the marketplace will accept PLEX alongside other supported payment methods.

PLEX can be transferred between any ERC20 compatible wallets, such as MetaMask, Parity and Mist. This allows PLEX to be freely traded on exchanges as well as being used on the marketplace. There will only be a maximum of 100,000,000 PLEX in circulation.

We are very passionate about the Community Investment Fund because it allows us to pay forward the generosity that our token sale investors have shown us, by giving back to the community.

Frequently asked Questions

  • add_circle

    What is the Storeplex marketplace?

    Storeplex is a decentralised marketplace that allows products and services to be purchased and sold for cryptocurrencies on a global scale.

  • add_circle

    What does decentralised mean?

    Decentralised means that the marketplace relies upon the Ethereum blockchain to function. This removes the necessity of having largely influential groups or organisations involved in the management of the project.

  • add_circle

    What can PLEX be used for?

    PLEX tokens can be used to buy and sell products or services on the marketplace, promote user listings, upgrade seller accounts, trade on exchange websites and fund small-scale businesses via the Community Investment Fund.

  • add_circle

    How many PLEX tokens will there be?

    There will be a total maximum supply of 100,000,000 (100 million) PLEX.

  • add_circle

    What other payment methods will be supported on the marketplace?

    There are currently plans to support Bitcoin, Ethereum, Bitcoin Cash, Litecoin, NEO, Monero and Dash. Other supported cryptocurrencies will be added at a later date, including ERC20 tokens.

  • add_circle

    How are product disputes resolved on the marketplace?

    If a buyer is unsatisfied with a transaction, they can dispute it in the ‘Resolution Centre’. This will allow both parties to provide relevant evidence concerning the transaction. A support member will then objectively rule the decision in one party’s favour.

  • add_circle

    What exchanges will PLEX be listed on?

    There are currently plans to list PLEX token on Bittrex, Binance, HitBTC, Cryptopia and Poloniex. More exchanges will be added at a later date.

  • add_circle

    How many PLEX tokens are available in the token sales?

    During the token pre-sale, a total of 10,000,000 (10 million) PLEX tokens will be available, and during the main token sale, a total of 40,000,000 (40 million) PLEX tokens will be available. The token pre-sale will contain a 100% token bonus whereas the main token sale will consist of four phases, each of which offering a varying token bonus.

  • add_circle

    What happens to PLEX tokens that are not purchased during the token sales?

    Any tokens that have not been purchased after the token sales will be burned. This means that the total supply of PLEX is subject to change.

  • add_circle

    What will attract users to the marketplace?

    Storeplex prides itself with having no insertion fees and no processing fees on transactions made using PLEX. This is very important to us as we believe that other marketplaces unnecessarily impose high fees on sellers, which can drastically reduce profits and drive sellers away from their platform.

    We have also implemented the Community Investment Fund which offers small-scale businesses the opportunity to expand in a largely competitive market. With very fair repayment terms, we aim to attract many small merchants via this initiative. In effect, the Storeplex marketplace will be saturated with a large variety of products and services.

  • add_circle

    How are transactions protected from fraudulent activity?

    By utilising Smart Contracts, we are able to protect both buyers and sellers from fraudulent activity. This is achieved by using an escrow for each transaction. The funds will not be released to the seller until the buyer has received the goods and is satisfied with the transaction. If a dispute arises, our trained support team will objectively rule the case in one party’s favour once evidence has been acquired from both sides. Additionally, blockchain transactions cannot fall victim to chargebacks and other fraudulent misuse.

  • add_circle

    How will you combat the volatility of cryptocurrency prices?

    When a product or service is listed on the Storeplex marketplace, the cryptocurrency price will be compared against the current trading value. This means that the cryptocurrency price will dynamically update to reflect the volatility of the markets. This results in a fair trading environment where neither party is greatly impacted by shifts in the market.

Instructions

To make PLEX tokens visible in your compatible wallet, you must configure it as a token. This can be achieved by navigating to the 'Add Token' section and entering these details.

  1. Open MetaMask and ensure that the 'Main Ethereum Network' is selected at the top.
  2. Click on the 'Tokens' tab.
  3. Click 'Add Token'.
  4. Enter the 'Contract address' from the right-hand side.
  5. Click 'Add' once the 'Token Symbol' and 'Decimals of Precision' fields have autofilled.
